NOW AI - Web Interface

IAAC - Master in Advanced Computation for Architecture & Design MaCAD - Final Thesis Project

Faculty: Gabriella Rossi (CITA).

Students: Felipe Romero & Jonathan Hernandez



This web app is part of the final thesis for the Masters in Advanced Computation for Architecture & Design at IAAC (Institute of Advanced Architecture of Catalonia).

The project develops a workflow between GIS & Grasshopper using open source data of Bogota (IDECA) and multipurpose survey done by Secretaria Distrital de Planeacion (SDP) & DANE in 2017. This webapp uses Rhino Compute to visualise the results in the cloud.

The project was developed by the students Felipe Romero & Jonathan Hernandez under the supervision of Gabriella Rossi (CITA).

We hope this project demonstrates the opportunity to create data-driven urban design policies that can benefit marginal communities. This map focus on USME, Bogota Urban District.



Now AI - Appserver GH definition that uses GIS data to visualise risk areas of the city of Bogota. This definition also proposes a new methodology to mitigate risk based on urban assumptions.
